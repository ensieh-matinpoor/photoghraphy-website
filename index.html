Photography projects

Tajrish

Tajrish Square, known as Sar-e Pol-e Tajrish, is one of the famous and important areas in the north of Tehran. I often visit Tajrish for shopping, walking, and most of the time, to enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the area. No matter what time of day you go there, you can feel the liveliness of the square and its surroundings.

Enqelab Street

Enghelab Street is located in the heart of the capital, Tehran, Iran. This street stretches along the east-west axis and spans approximately 5 kilometers, making it one of the longest streets in the city. In fact, Enghelab Street is about one-third the length of Valiasr Street. The primary reason for constructing this street was to create an access route to the University of Tehran, which is considered one of the first and most prestigious universities in Iran.

From my window

Everything shut down; we should stay at home. The new virus spreads quickly. Everyone is getting bored as they can't come outside, and all activities have come to a standstill. My window became a good place to spend time and have fun. During the pandemic,I brought my camera and started shooting photos from my window.

If they weren't blocked

This project was formed during the time of pandemic and quarantine. Those days, standing by the window and looking outside at the sky was one of the fascinating pastimes for me. The changing colors of the sky and the swift movement of clouds captivated me. I started taking photos of the sky.
