Ensieh Matinpoor

Photography is not my job, it's my passion

I'm Ensieh Matinpoor, an amateur photographer with a talent for finding beauty in unexpected places. From the vibrant streets of Tajrish to the forgotten corners of Birmingham, my lens captures the stunning and the mundane alike. Follow my journey as I share my unique perspective through stunning images of rubbish and the overlooked.

Photography projects

Tajrish

Tajrish Square, known as Sar-e Pol-e Tajrish, is one of the famous and important areas in the north of Tehran. I often visit Tajrish for shopping, walking, and most of the time, to enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of the area. No matter what time of day you go there, you can feel the liveliness of the square and its surroundings. Initially, I started this project as something easy to access and lively, but after spending time going back and forth, I began to perceive things differently.
