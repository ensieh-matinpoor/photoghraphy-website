Tajrish

Tajrish Square, or Sar-e Pol-e Tajrish, situated in northern Tehran, is a vibrant street. A photographer's paradise. Its perpetual liveliness, day or night, offers an ever-changing tapestry of life. The square's colorful markets, spice vendors,... a visually striking backdrop. The diverse mix of locals and tourists, from the elderly conversing to younger generations, presents an array of candid moments rich in human experience. Tajrish's architectural gems, such as the Tajrish Bazaar and Imamzadeh Saleh shrine, provide picturesque settings. Street photographers in Tajrish capture not just images but the essence of a place where life's stories unfold.
