Enqelab Street

Enghelab Street is located in the heart of the capital, Tehran, Iran. This street stretches along the east-west axis and spans approximately 5 kilometers, making it one of the longest streets in the city. In fact, Enghelab Street is about one-third the length of Valiasr Street. The primary reason for constructing this street was to create an access route to the University of Tehran, which is considered one of the first and most prestigious universities in Iran.
